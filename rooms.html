OUR ROOMS

1 Beds Room

Experience comfort and convenience in our hotel's cozy one-bedroom room. Relax in a plush king-sized bed, enjoy modern amenities like a flat-screen TV and complimentary Wi-Fi, and refresh in the stylish en-suite bathroom. Our friendly staff is available around the clock to cater to your needs. Make the most of your stay in our inviting and well-appointed one-bedroom room.

2 Beds Room

Enjoy the comfort of our two-bedroom room. Whether you're traveling with family or friends, this spacious room offers two comfortable beds to accommodate your needs. Relax and unwind in a cozy environment featuring modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV and complimentary Wi-Fi. The en-suite bathroom is well-appointed with all the essentials for your convenience.

Luxurious suite

Indulge in the epitome of luxury in our hotel's lavish suite featuring three beds and a fully equipped kitchen. This opulent suite is designed to cater to your every need, providing an abundance of space and comfort. The elegantly furnished bedroom showcases three plush beds with high-quality linens, ensuring a restful night's sleep for you and your companions. The en-suite bathroom is a luxurious sanctuary, featuring a lavish shower, a deep soaking tub, and premium bath amenities. It's a perfect place to unwind and rejuvenate after a long day.
